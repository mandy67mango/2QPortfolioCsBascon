⸸ Pochacco ⸸

Pochacco (ポチャッコ, Pochakko) is a character of Sanrio, designed by Minoru Onoue. He lives on Uguisu Alley on the outskirts of Fuwafuwa Town and was born on February 29th, on a leap year. Pochacco is a white dog with black floppy ears, black nose, eyes, and paw pads, as well as a short tail with a black tip. He often wears red or blue t-shirts or hoodies.

Originally known as "The Yorimichi Dog," Pochacco is a curious and clumsy boy who sometimes sticks his nose in where it isn’t wanted. He is a vegetarian and his favorite food is banana ice cream. He enjoys walking, though he has trouble walking straight and often totters from side to side. Pochacco is also athletic and proficient in sports, especially soccer.
